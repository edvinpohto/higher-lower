Higher or Lower?

In this game, a card will be drawn and you will have to guess whether the next card will be higher or lower than the one showed.

The cards used are standard ranking numerical cards, with an Ace low and King high.

For each correct guess you get a point and the game is won by guessing right all the way through the deck of cards.

Good luck!
